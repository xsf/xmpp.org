We're very excited to announce our presence at the coming [FOSDEM 2026](https://fosdem.org)!

Once again, many members of the XMPP community will be attending, and we hope to see you there!

## Realtime Lounge

As usual, we will have the *Realtime Lounge* set up, where you can come and meet community members, project developers, see demos and ask us questions.

This year we will be at the regular place on the [K building's 2nd floor](https://fosdem.org/2026/stands/).

{{< figure src="/images/blog/realtime-lounge-location.png" caption="Map of the K building level 2" >}}

## Decentralised Communication Devroom & Talks

Be invited to the technical talks around decentralised communication.
There will also be talks on various messaging and RTC projects such as ActivityPub, ATProto, Automerge, DeltaChat, Matrix and of course XMPP in the [Decentralised Communication](https://fosdem.org/2026/schedule/track/decentralised-communication/) developer room.

To become a speaker please submit your talk through the [FOSDEM's conference management system](https://pretalx.fosdem.org/fosdem-2026/cfp).
You can read more here: [Call for Participation to the FOSDEM 2026 Decentralised Communications Devroom](https://matrix.org/blog/2025/11/fosdem-cfp/).
**Sumission deadline is 30th November 2025.**
Attendees, speakers, and volunteers are expected to follow the [FOSDEM Code of Conduct](https://fosdem.org/2026/practical/conduct/) at all times.

### Info & Dates

* **When?**           31st January + 1st February 2026

* ** Where exactly?**  [K building, 2nd floor](https://fosdem.org/2026/stands/)
* **Deadline talks?** [30th Novemver 2025](https://pretalx.fosdem.org/fosdem-2026/cfp)
* **Contact?**        [XMPP Community chats](https://xmpp.org/community/chat/) & [XMPP Community mailing lists](https://xmpp.org/community/mailing-lists/)


## XMPP Summit 28 prio to FOSDEM 2026

Prio to FOSDEM, the XSF will also traditionally hold its [28th XMPP summit](https://wiki.xmpp.org/web/Conferences/Summit_25).
This is where community members gather to discuss protocol changes and the XMPP roadmap.
Everyone is welcome to join, just kindly list yourself free of cost as we have limited space.
Check our online presence and communication, too (see our media links in the footer).

We are looking forward to see you there!
