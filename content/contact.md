There are a number of ways to contact the XMPP Standards Foundation or its members.

- **General enquiry**: For general enquiries please email [info@xmpp.org](mailto:info@xmpp.org). If you prefer to contact someone directly, many of the [XSF Board and XMPP Council members](/about/xmpp-standards-foundation) list contact details in their profiles.

- **Chat & mailing lists**: Finally, you can join one of many [XMPP-related discussions](/community/chat) by chat or by using the mailing lists.

- **Sponsorship opportunities**: For [sponsorship opportunities](/community/sponsorship/) please email [sponsors@xmpp.org](mailto:sponsors@xmpp.org).

- **Licensing**: If you have any questions about the [Jabber Trademark Licensing Program](/about/xsf/jabber-trademark), please contact [trademark@jabber.org](mailto:trademark@jabber.org).
